<p>The <i><b>Mohave Valley Daily News</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Newspaper" title="Newspaper">newspaper</a> in <a href="/wiki/Bullhead_City,_Arizona" title="Bullhead City, Arizona">Bullhead City</a>, <a href="/wiki/Arizona" title="Arizona">Arizona</a>, <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-mondo_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-mondo-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> It was started in 1964 by Lee B. Perry, then owner and publisher of the nearby <i>Needles Desert Star</i>, a weekly newspaper serving <a href="/wiki/Needles,_California" title="Needles, California">Needles, California</a> and the southern <a href="/wiki/Mohave_Valley" title="Mohave Valley">Mohave Valley</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> It is owned by <a href="/w/index.php?title=Brehm_Communications&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Brehm Communications (page does not exist)">Brehm Communications</a>, and it has a circulation of 8,600.<sup id="cite_ref-mondo_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-mondo-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
