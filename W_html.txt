<p>This is a <b>list of newspapers in <a href="/wiki/Colorado" title="Colorado">Colorado</a></b>.  According to the <a href="/wiki/Library_of_Congress" title="Library of Congress">Library of Congress</a>, over 2,500 newspapers have been published in Colorado.  The first documented newspaper was the <i>Rocky Mountain Gold Reporter and Mountain City Herald</i>, which was published by T. Gibson in 1859 in <a href="/wiki/Mountain_City,_Colorado" class="mw-redirect" title="Mountain City, Colorado">Mountain City, Colorado</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-LOC_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LOC-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>   
</p>
