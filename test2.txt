	<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en"><i>Kstati</i></h1>

		<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><i><b>Kstati Russian-American Newspaper</b></i> or Apropos-Kstati, is a weekly <a href="/wiki/Russian-language" class="mw-redirect" title="Russian-language">Russian-language</a> <a href="/wiki/Newspaper" title="Newspaper">newspaper</a> published in the <a href="/wiki/San_Francisco_Bay" title="San Francisco Bay">San Francisco Bay</a> Area.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The <a href="/wiki/Editor" class="mw-redirect" title="Editor">editors</a> are Nikolay Sundeyev<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> and Janna Sundeyeva.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> It was created in 1994<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> and has a circulation of 9,000.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
